Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) - Government Jobs

CISF is a premier security force in India responsible for safeguarding critical infrastructure, government facilities, and public properties. It offers diverse career opportunities in security, administration, and technical roles. Freshers earn ₹3–6 lakh, mid-level professionals ₹6–15 lakh, and senior officers can earn ₹15–30 lakh annually.
