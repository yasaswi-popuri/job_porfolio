Corporate Finance Field

Corporate Finance involves managing a company's financial activities, including budgeting, capital structure, investments, and financial planning. These fields are critical for driving profitability, growth, and long-term sustainability. Freshers earn ₹4–10 lakh, mid-level ₹10–25 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹25–70 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹1.5 crore annually.

Available Roles

Financial Analyst

Analyze financial data, prepare reports, and support decision-making processes.

Finance Manager

Oversee financial operations, budgeting, and strategic planning for organizations.

Treasury Analyst

Manage cash flow, liquidity, and risk management for businesses.

Investor Relations Specialist

Communicate with investors and stakeholders to maintain transparency and trust.

Cost Accountant

Analyze costs, optimize budgets, and improve financial efficiency.

Corporate Development Manager

Lead mergers, acquisitions, and strategic partnerships for growth.

Risk Manager

Evaluate financial risks and develop strategies to mitigate them.

Capital Budgeting Analyst

Evaluate investment opportunities and allocate resources effectively.

Internal Auditor

Assess internal controls, compliance, and operational efficiency.

Financial Controller

Oversee accounting functions, financial reporting, and regulatory compliance.

Business Analyst

Analyze business performance and recommend improvements for profitability.

Strategic Planner

Develop long-term financial strategies to achieve organizational goals.
