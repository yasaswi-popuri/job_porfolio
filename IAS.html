Indian Administrative Service (IAS)

The Indian Administrative Service (IAS) is one of the premier civil services in India, responsible for managing government affairs, implementing policies, and ensuring public welfare. IAS officers serve in various capacities, from district administration to central government roles. Starting salaries range from ₹56,100–₹1,32,000 per month, with significant perks and allowances.
