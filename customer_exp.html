Customer Experience & Support

Customer Experience & Support focuses on delivering exceptional service to customers, ensuring satisfaction, loyalty, and trust. This field involves resolving issues, gathering feedback, and improving service delivery. Freshers earn ₹2–6 lakh, mid-level professionals ₹6–15 lakh, and experienced roles can exceed ₹30 lakh annually.

Available Roles

Customer Support Specialist

Assist customers with queries, complaints, and technical issues via phone, email, or chat.

Customer Success Manager

Ensure customers achieve their goals using your product or service.

Technical Support Engineer

Provide technical assistance and troubleshoot product-related issues for customers.

Feedback Analyst

Analyze customer feedback to identify areas for improvement in products or services.

Chatbot Developer

Design and implement AI-powered chatbots to enhance customer support efficiency.

Service Quality Manager

Monitor and improve the quality of customer service interactions across teams.

Support Team Leader

Lead and train customer support teams to deliver excellent service.

Account Manager

Maintain relationships with key clients and ensure their needs are met.

Customer Retention Specialist

Develop strategies to retain customers and reduce churn rates.

Community Manager

Engage with customers on social media and online forums to build brand loyalty.

Complaint Resolution Officer

Handle escalated complaints and resolve them to ensure customer satisfaction.

User Experience (UX) Researcher

Conduct research to understand customer pain points and improve service delivery.
