Defense Services (Army, Navy, Air Force)

Defense services encompass careers in the Army, Navy, and Air Force, offering opportunities to serve the nation while developing leadership, discipline, and technical skills. Freshers can earn ₹6–12 lakh annually, mid-level officers ₹12–25 lakh, and senior officers ₹25–50 lakh or more. Specialized roles and ranks come with additional benefits and allowances.
