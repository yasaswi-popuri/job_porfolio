Special Education Field

Special Education involves creating inclusive learning environments and tailored educational plans for students with disabilities or unique learning needs. These fields are critical for fostering equity, accessibility, and personal growth for all learners. Freshers earn ₹3–7 lakh, mid-level ₹7–12 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹12–25 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹40 lakh annually.

Available Roles

Special Education Teacher

Teach and support students with disabilities or learning challenges in inclusive classrooms.

Educational Therapist

Provide specialized interventions to address learning difficulties and developmental delays.

Behavior Analyst

Assess and develop strategies to improve behavioral outcomes for students with special needs.

Speech-Language Pathologist

Support students with speech, language, and communication disorders.

Occupational Therapist

Help students develop fine motor skills and perform daily activities independently.

Learning Support Coordinator

Oversee programs and resources to support students with special educational needs.

Inclusive Education Specialist

Design and implement strategies to promote inclusive learning environments.

Psychologist (Special Education)

Assess and address psychological and emotional needs of students with disabilities.

Assistive Technology Specialist

Integrate technology tools to support students with disabilities in learning.

Parent Liaison (Special Education)

Collaborate with parents and caregivers to support their child's educational journey.

IEP Coordinator

Develop and manage Individualized Education Programs (IEPs) for students with special needs.

Rehabilitation Counselor

Support students with disabilities in achieving personal, academic, and career goals.
