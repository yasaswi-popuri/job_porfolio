IT Support & Networking Field

IT Support and Networking involve managing hardware, software, and network infrastructure to ensure smooth operations. These fields are critical for maintaining connectivity, troubleshooting issues, and supporting users. Freshers earn ₹2–6 lakh, mid-level ₹6–12 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹12–25 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹40 lakh annually.

Available Roles

IT Support Specialist

Provide technical assistance and resolve hardware/software issues for users.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in IT or related field.
  • Proficiency in troubleshooting hardware and software issues.
  • Strong communication and customer service skills.
  • Certifications like CompTIA A+ or ITIL are preferred.

Network Administrator

Manage and maintain network infrastructure, including routers and switches.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Network Engineering or related field.
  • Experience with configuring and troubleshooting networks.
  • Proficiency in network protocols (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP).
  • Certifications like CCNA or Network+ are preferred.

Help Desk Technician

Address user queries and provide first-level technical support.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Diploma or Bachelor’s degree in IT or related field.
  • Experience in resolving technical issues via phone or chat.
  • Strong problem-solving and interpersonal skills.
  • Certifications like HDI Support Center Analyst are a plus.

Systems Administrator

Oversee server management, backups, and system configurations.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in IT or Computer Science.
  • Experience with server operating systems (Windows Server, Linux).
  • Proficiency in scripting languages (PowerShell, Bash).
  • Certifications like MCSE or RHCSA are preferred.

Network Engineer

Design, implement, and troubleshoot enterprise-level networks.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Network Engineering or related field.
  • Experience with network design and implementation.
  • Proficiency in routing, switching, and firewalls.
  • Certifications like CCNP or Juniper are preferred.

IT Manager

Lead IT teams and oversee technology strategy and implementation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in IT Management or related field.
  • Experience in managing IT teams and projects.
  • Strong leadership and decision-making skills.
  • Certifications like PMP or ITIL Expert are preferred.

Cybersecurity Analyst

Protect networks and systems from cyber threats and vulnerabilities.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or related field.
  • Experience with security tools (SIEM, IDS/IPS).
  • Strong understanding of cybersecurity frameworks.
  • Certifications like CISSP or CEH are preferred.

Cloud Support Engineer

Provide technical support for cloud-based services and platforms.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in IT or Cloud Computing.
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Proficiency in troubleshooting cloud infrastructure issues.
  • Certifications like AWS Certified Solutions Architect are preferred.

Telecom Engineer

Install and maintain telecommunications systems, including VoIP and PBX.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Telecommunications or Electrical Engineering.
  • Experience with VoIP, PBX, and SIP technologies.
  • Strong knowledge of telecom protocols and standards.
  • Certifications like CCNA Voice are a plus.

Data Center Technician

Manage data center operations, including servers and cooling systems.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Diploma or Bachelor’s degree in IT or related field.
  • Experience with server rack installation and maintenance.
  • Knowledge of power and cooling systems in data centers.
  • Certifications like Data Center Specialist are preferred.

IT Auditor

Evaluate IT systems for compliance, security, and operational efficiency.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in IT Audit or related field.
  • Experience in auditing IT systems and processes.
  • Strong analytical and reporting skills.
  • Certifications like CISA or CISM are preferred.

Desktop Support Engineer

Troubleshoot and repair desktops, laptops, and peripherals for end-users.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Diploma or Bachelor’s degree in IT or related field.
  • Experience in repairing hardware and software issues.
  • Strong troubleshooting and communication skills.
  • Certifications like CompTIA A+ are preferred.
