Cybersecurity & Ethical Hacking Field

Cybersecurity and Ethical Hacking involve protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber threats. These fields are critical for ensuring privacy, security, and compliance in the digital age. Freshers earn ₹4–12 lakh, mid-level ₹12–25 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹25–60 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹1 crore annually.

Available Roles

Cybersecurity Analyst

Monitor systems for vulnerabilities and respond to security incidents.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or related field.
  • Proficiency in security tools (SIEM, IDS/IPS).
  • Understanding of network protocols and firewalls.
  • Certifications like CompTIA Security+ or CEH are a plus.

Ethical Hacker

Identify and exploit system vulnerabilities to strengthen security.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in IT or Cybersecurity.
  • Experience with penetration testing tools (Metasploit, Burp Suite).
  • Strong understanding of ethical hacking principles.
  • Certifications like CEH or OSCP are preferred.

Security Architect

Design and implement secure network architectures.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Cybersecurity or related field.
  • Experience with firewall configuration and encryption protocols.
  • Strong knowledge of security frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001).
  • Certifications like CISSP or CISM are highly valued.

Penetration Tester

Simulate cyberattacks to evaluate system defenses.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or related field.
  • Proficiency in vulnerability assessment tools (Nmap, Nessus).
  • Experience in manual and automated penetration testing.
  • Certifications like OSCP or GPEN are a plus.

Incident Responder

Investigate and mitigate security breaches and attacks.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or related field.
  • Experience with incident response frameworks (SANS, NIST).
  • Proficiency in forensic tools (FTK, EnCase).
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Security Consultant

Advise organizations on best practices for cybersecurity.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or related field.
  • Experience in risk assessment and mitigation strategies.
  • Strong communication and presentation skills.
  • Certifications like CISSP or CISA are preferred.

Network Security Engineer

Secure network infrastructure and manage firewalls.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Network Engineering or related field.
  • Proficiency in configuring firewalls and intrusion detection systems.
  • Experience with network monitoring tools (SolarWinds, Nagios).
  • Certifications like CCNA Security or Juniper are a plus.

Cloud Security Specialist

Protect cloud-based systems and applications from threats.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or Cloud Computing.
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Proficiency in cloud security tools (AWS Shield, Azure Security Center).
  • Certifications like CCSP or AWS Certified Security are preferred.

Malware Analyst

Analyze malicious software and develop countermeasures.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or Computer Science.
  • Experience with malware analysis tools (IDA Pro, Ghidra).
  • Strong reverse engineering skills.
  • Certifications like GREM or GCIH are a plus.

Forensic Analyst

Investigate cybercrimes and recover digital evidence.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or Forensics.
  • Proficiency in forensic tools (FTK, Autopsy).
  • Experience in handling digital evidence and legal procedures.
  • Certifications like CHFI or GCFA are preferred.

Compliance Auditor

Ensure adherence to cybersecurity regulations and standards.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or Auditing.
  • Experience in auditing frameworks (ISO 27001, GDPR).
  • Strong attention to detail and documentation skills.
  • Certifications like CISA or CRISC are highly valued.

Security Operations Manager

Oversee security teams and manage incident response strategies.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or related field.
  • Experience in managing security operations centers (SOC).
  • Strong leadership and communication skills.
  • Certifications like CISSP or CISM are preferred.
