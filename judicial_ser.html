Judicial Services for Government Jobs

Judicial services involve interpreting and applying laws to ensure justice, resolve disputes, and uphold the legal framework of society. These roles are critical in maintaining law and order, protecting rights, and ensuring fair governance. Freshers earn ₹6–12 lakh, mid-level professionals ₹12–30 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹30–80 lakh. Senior judges and legal advisors can earn over ₹1 crore annually.
