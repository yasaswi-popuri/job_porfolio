UI/UX Design Field

UI/UX Design involves creating visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces for websites, applications, and digital products. These fields focus on enhancing user experience, accessibility, and usability. Freshers earn ₹3–8 lakh, mid-level ₹8–15 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹15–30 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹50 lakh annually.

Available Roles

UI Designer

Design visually appealing interfaces for websites and applications.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Design or related field.
  • Proficiency in design tools (Adobe XD, Figma, Sketch).
  • Strong understanding of typography, color theory, and layout.
  • Portfolio showcasing UI design projects.

UX Designer

Create intuitive and user-centered designs to enhance usability.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Design, Psychology, or related field.
  • Experience with wireframing and prototyping tools (Figma, InVision).
  • Strong problem-solving and research skills.
  • Understanding of user-centered design principles.

Interaction Designer

Focus on designing interactive elements for seamless user experiences.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Design or related field.
  • Experience with interaction design tools (Proto.io, Axure).
  • Knowledge of micro-interactions and animations.
  • Strong portfolio highlighting interactive designs.

Visual Designer

Develop visual concepts and assets for digital products.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design or related field.
  • Proficiency in graphic design tools (Photoshop, Illustrator).
  • Strong sense of aesthetics and creativity.
  • Experience with branding and visual storytelling.

Product Designer

Combine UI/UX principles to design end-to-end product experiences.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Design or related field.
  • Experience in full-cycle product design (research to implementation).
  • Strong collaboration skills with cross-functional teams.
  • Portfolio showcasing product design work.

UX Researcher

Conduct user research to inform design decisions and improve usability.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Psychology, Human-Computer Interaction, or related field.
  • Experience with qualitative and quantitative research methods.
  • Proficiency in usability testing tools (Maze, UserTesting).
  • Strong analytical and communication skills.

Information Architect

Organize content and structure information for clarity and accessibility.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Information Science or related field.
  • Experience in organizing complex information systems.
  • Proficiency in sitemap and taxonomy creation tools.
  • Strong understanding of user navigation patterns.

UI/UX Developer

Bridge design and development by implementing UI/UX designs into code.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Experience with front-end frameworks (React, Angular).
  • Strong understanding of responsive design principles.

Graphic Designer

Create visual elements like logos, icons, and illustrations for digital platforms.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design or related field.
  • Proficiency in design tools (Illustrator, CorelDRAW).
  • Strong portfolio showcasing creative work.
  • Experience with branding and marketing materials.

Motion Designer

Add animations and transitions to enhance user interactions.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Animation or related field.
  • Experience with motion design tools (After Effects, Principle).
  • Strong understanding of timing and easing in animations.
  • Portfolio showcasing motion design projects.

Usability Tester

Evaluate designs through user testing to ensure functionality and accessibility.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Design, Psychology, or related field.
  • Experience with usability testing methodologies.
  • Proficiency in testing tools (Hotjar, Crazy Egg).
  • Strong attention to detail and analytical skills.

Design Systems Specialist

Create and maintain reusable design systems for consistency across products.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Design or related field.
  • Experience in building and maintaining design systems.
  • Proficiency in design tools (Figma, Storybook).
  • Strong understanding of modular design principles.
