Financial Planning & Wealth Management Field

Financial Planning and Wealth Management involve helping individuals and businesses achieve their financial goals. These fields focus on investment strategies, retirement planning, tax optimization, and estate management. Freshers earn ₹4–10 lakh, mid-level ₹10–25 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹25–50 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹1 crore annually.

Available Roles

Financial Planner

Create personalized financial plans to help clients achieve their goals.

Wealth Manager

Provide comprehensive wealth management services for high-net-worth clients.

Investment Advisor

Guide clients on investment opportunities and portfolio management.

Retirement Planner

Develop retirement strategies to ensure financial security in later years.

Tax Consultant

Advise clients on tax planning and compliance to minimize liabilities.

Estate Planner

Help clients plan the transfer of wealth and assets to heirs.

Portfolio Manager

Manage investment portfolios to maximize returns and minimize risks.

Risk Manager

Assess and mitigate financial risks for individuals and businesses.

Insurance Advisor

Recommend insurance products to protect clients' financial interests.

Private Banker

Offer exclusive banking services to high-net-worth individuals.

Financial Analyst

Analyze financial data to guide investment and business decisions.

Trust Officer

Manage trusts and estates on behalf of clients and beneficiaries.
