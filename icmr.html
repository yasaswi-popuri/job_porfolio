Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) Roles

The Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) is the apex body for the formulation, coordination, and promotion of biomedical research in India.

Available Roles

Research Scientist

Conduct cutting-edge biomedical research to address India's health challenges.

Go to

Medical Officer

Provide medical expertise in research studies and clinical trials across various medical fields.

Go to

Public Health Expert

Contribute to public health research, policy development, and implementation strategies for disease prevention.

Go to

Laboratory Technician

Support scientific research by performing diagnostic tests and experiments in ICMR laboratories.

Go to

Clinical Research Associate

Oversee and coordinate clinical trials and research studies to improve healthcare outcomes.

Go to

Biostatistician

Analyze and interpret data from clinical and epidemiological studies to inform health policies and programs.

Go to

Project Manager

Manage and coordinate research projects funded by ICMR, ensuring timely execution and compliance.

Go to

Data Analyst

Process and analyze large sets of health data to identify trends and inform research outcomes.

Go to

Health Communication Specialist

Develop and implement health communication strategies to spread awareness on various health issues.

Go to

Quality Assurance Specialist

Ensure quality standards in biomedical research and clinical trials conducted by ICMR.

Go to

Ethics Committee Member

Evaluate research proposals for ethical considerations and ensure compliance with ethical standards in biomedical research.

Go to

Pharmacologist

Study the effects of drugs and chemicals on biological systems and contribute to the development of new treatments.
