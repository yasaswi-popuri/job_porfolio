Indian Railway Personnel Service (IRPS)

The Indian Railway Personnel Service is a Group A central service under the Ministry of Railways. It offers diverse career opportunities in human resource management, administration, recruitment, training, and welfare of railway employees. Freshers can earn ₹8–15 lakh annually, while experienced professionals can earn ₹20–40 lakh or more.

Available Roles

Oversee recruitment, training, and employee welfare programs within the Indian Railways.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Degree in Human Resources or related field.
  • Experience in HR management and labor relations.
  • Strong leadership and communication skills.
