Software and Development Field

The Software and Development field creates software, websites, and apps. Developers write code, fix bugs, and ensure security. With growing technology, demand is high. Freshers earn ₹3.5–8 lakh, mid-level ₹8–15 lakh, and experienced ₹15–25 lakh. Senior roles can earn up to ₹50 lakh. This field offers great pay and job security.
