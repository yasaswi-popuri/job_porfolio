Indian Railway Accounts Service (IRAS)

The Indian Railway Accounts Service is a Group A central service under the Ministry of Railways. It offers exciting career opportunities in financial management, auditing, taxation, and budgeting for the railways. Freshers can earn ₹8–15 lakh annually, while experienced professionals can earn ₹20–40 lakh or more.

Available Roles

Accounts Officer

Manage financial accounts, budgets, and expenditures for railway operations.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Commerce, Finance, or equivalent.
  • Experience in financial accounting or auditing.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Knowledge of railway financial regulations.
