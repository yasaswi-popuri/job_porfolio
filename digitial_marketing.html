Digital Marketing & SEO

Digital Marketing and SEO focus on promoting businesses online through strategies like search engine optimization, social media marketing, and content creation. This field drives brand awareness, customer engagement, and revenue growth. Freshers earn ₹3–8 lakh, mid-level professionals ₹8–20 lakh, and experienced roles can exceed ₹50 lakh annually.

Available Roles

SEO Specialist

Optimize websites to improve search engine rankings and drive organic traffic.

Social Media Manager

Create and manage content on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.

Content Writer

Write engaging blogs, articles, and website copy to attract and inform audiences.

Email Marketing Specialist

Design and execute email campaigns to nurture leads and boost conversions.

PPC Specialist

Manage pay-per-click campaigns on Google Ads and other platforms.

Digital Marketing Analyst

Analyze data to measure campaign performance and optimize strategies.

Copywriter

Craft persuasive ad copies and slogans to enhance brand messaging.

Marketing Automation Specialist

Set up automated workflows for lead generation and customer retention.

Brand Strategist

Develop strategies to build and promote a strong brand identity.

Influencer Marketing Manager

Collaborate with influencers to promote products and services.

E-commerce Specialist

Optimize online stores and campaigns for higher sales and customer satisfaction.

Video Marketing Specialist

Create and promote video content for YouTube, TikTok, and other platforms.
