Data Science & AI

Data Science and Artificial Intelligence involve analyzing data, building predictive models, and developing intelligent systems. These fields are in high demand due to their applications in healthcare, finance, marketing, and more. Freshers earn ₹4–10 lakh, mid-level ₹10–20 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹20–50 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹1 crore annually.
