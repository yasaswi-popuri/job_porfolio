Web3 Field

Web3 involves decentralized technologies like blockchain, smart contracts, and cryptocurrencies. These fields are revolutionizing industries by enabling trustless systems, decentralized finance (DeFi), and digital ownership through NFTs. Freshers earn ₹4–12 lakh, mid-level ₹12–25 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹25–60 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹1 crore annually.

Available Roles

Blockchain Developer

Build decentralized applications (dApps) and smart contracts using platforms like Ethereum.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Proficiency in blockchain platforms (Ethereum, Solana).
  • Experience with programming languages (Solidity, Rust).
  • Certifications like Certified Blockchain Developer are preferred.

Smart Contract Engineer

Design, develop, and audit smart contracts for blockchain-based projects.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in IT or related field.
  • Strong understanding of Solidity and smart contract security.
  • Experience with auditing tools and vulnerability testing.
  • Certifications like Certified Smart Contract Auditor are preferred.

Crypto Analyst

Analyze cryptocurrency markets and trends to guide investment strategies.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance or Economics.
  • Experience in financial analysis and market research.
  • Strong knowledge of blockchain ecosystems and DeFi protocols.
  • Certifications like Cryptocurrency Certification Program are preferred.

NFT Developer

Create and manage non-fungible tokens (NFTs) for digital assets and collectibles.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in IT or related field.
  • Proficiency in NFT standards (ERC-721, ERC-1155).
  • Experience with blockchain platforms like Ethereum and Polygon.
  • Certifications like Certified NFT Developer are preferred.

DeFi Specialist

Work on decentralized finance protocols, including lending, borrowing, and staking platforms.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance or IT-related field.
  • Experience with DeFi protocols (Uniswap, Aave, Compound).
  • Strong understanding of yield farming and liquidity pools.
  • Certifications like Certified DeFi Expert are preferred.

Blockchain Architect

Design scalable and secure blockchain solutions for enterprises.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in IT or related field.
  • Experience in designing distributed systems and consensus algorithms.
  • Strong knowledge of cryptography and fault tolerance.
  • Certifications like Certified Blockchain Architect are preferred.

DAO Manager

Oversee decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs) and their governance processes.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business or IT-related field.
  • Experience in managing DAOs and community engagement.
  • Strong understanding of governance models and voting mechanisms.
  • Certifications like Certified DAO Manager are preferred.

Web3 Security Auditor

Identify vulnerabilities in smart contracts and decentralized applications.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity or related field.
  • Experience with security audits and penetration testing.
  • Strong knowledge of blockchain vulnerabilities (reentrancy, integer overflow).
  • Certifications like Certified Blockchain Security Professional are preferred.

Crypto Product Manager

Lead product development for blockchain-based applications and platforms.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business or IT-related field.
  • Experience in product management and agile methodologies.
  • Strong understanding of blockchain use cases and user needs.
  • Certifications like Certified Product Manager are preferred.

Tokenomics Designer

Develop token economies and incentive models for blockchain projects.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Economics or Finance.
  • Experience in designing token distribution models and incentives.
  • Strong understanding of game theory and behavioral economics.
  • Certifications like Certified Tokenomics Designer are preferred.

Layer 2 Developer

Work on scaling solutions like rollups and sidechains for blockchains.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Experience with Layer 2 solutions (Optimism, Arbitrum, zk-Rollups).
  • Strong knowledge of scalability challenges and solutions.
  • Certifications like Certified Layer 2 Developer are preferred.

Web3 UX/UI Designer

Create user-friendly interfaces for decentralized applications and wallets.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Design or related field.
  • Experience in designing intuitive interfaces for Web3 platforms.
  • Strong understanding of user experience principles.
  • Certifications like Certified UX Designer are preferred.
