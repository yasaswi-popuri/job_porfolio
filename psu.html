Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) - Government Jobs

Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) are government-owned corporations or enterprises that play a vital role in India's economic development. These organizations offer diverse career opportunities across various sectors such as energy, banking, transportation, and more. Freshers can expect salaries ranging from ₹4–10 lakh annually, while experienced professionals can earn ₹20–50 lakh or more.
