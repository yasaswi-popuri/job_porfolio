Regulatory Affairs & Quality Control

Regulatory Affairs and Quality Control focus on ensuring compliance with laws, regulations, and standards in industries like pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and food safety. This field plays a critical role in product safety, quality assurance, and regulatory submissions. Freshers earn ₹3–8 lakh, mid-level professionals ₹8–20 lakh, and experienced roles can exceed ₹50 lakh annually.

Available Roles

Regulatory Affairs Specialist

Prepare and submit regulatory documents for product approvals.

Quality Assurance Manager

Ensure products meet quality standards and compliance requirements.

Compliance Officer

Monitor adherence to regulatory guidelines and internal policies.

Validation Engineer

Test and validate equipment and processes to meet regulatory standards.

Documentation Specialist

Create and maintain accurate records for regulatory submissions.

Quality Control Analyst

Test and inspect products to ensure they meet quality specifications.

Regulatory Writer

Write technical documents for regulatory filings and submissions.

Product Safety Specialist

Evaluate and ensure the safety of products for consumer use.

Auditor

Conduct internal and external audits to ensure compliance.

Risk Management Specialist

Identify and mitigate risks related to regulatory compliance.

Labeling Compliance Specialist

Ensure product labeling meets regulatory and legal requirements.

Pharmacovigilance Officer

Monitor and report adverse effects of pharmaceutical products.
