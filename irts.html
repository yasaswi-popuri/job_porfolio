Indian Railway Traffic Service (IRTS)

The Indian Railway Traffic Service is a Group A central service under the Ministry of Railways. It offers exciting career opportunities in railway operations, transportation management, logistics, and passenger services. Freshers can earn ₹8–15 lakh annually, while experienced professionals can earn ₹20–40 lakh or more.

Available Roles

Train Operations Manager

Oversee the scheduling, movement, and safety of trains across the network.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering or equivalent.
  • Experience in railway operations or logistics.
  • Strong leadership and decision-making skills.
