Data Analytics in E-Commerce

Data Analytics in E-Commerce focuses on leveraging data to optimize customer experiences, improve sales, and enhance operational efficiency. This field involves analyzing trends, forecasting demand, and personalizing marketing strategies. Freshers earn ₹4–10 lakh, mid-level professionals ₹10–25 lakh, and experienced roles can exceed ₹50 lakh annually.

Available Roles

Data Analyst

Analyze e-commerce data to identify trends, customer behavior, and performance metrics.

Business Intelligence Specialist

Create dashboards and reports to provide actionable insights for decision-making.

Marketing Analyst

Evaluate the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and recommend improvements.

Predictive Modeler

Build models to forecast demand, customer churn, and sales trends.

Data Scientist

Use machine learning and statistical analysis to solve complex business problems.

Product Analytics Manager

Analyze product performance and customer feedback to guide product development.

Customer Insights Analyst

Understand customer preferences and behaviors to drive personalization strategies.

Supply Chain Analyst

Analyze supply chain data to optimize inventory management and logistics.

Conversion Rate Optimization Specialist

Improve website performance and increase sales through data-driven strategies.

A/B Testing Analyst

Design and analyze A/B tests to optimize website features and user experiences.

Data Visualization Expert

Create interactive visualizations to communicate complex data insights effectively.

Fraud Detection Analyst

Identify and prevent fraudulent activities using advanced analytics techniques.
