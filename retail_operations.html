Retail Operations

Retail Operations involve managing day-to-day activities in retail businesses, including inventory management, customer service, sales strategies, and store operations. These fields are critical for ensuring smooth operations, enhancing customer satisfaction, and driving profitability. Freshers earn ₹3–8 lakh, mid-level ₹8–15 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹15–30 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹50 lakh annually.

Available Roles

Retail Store Manager

Oversee daily operations, staff management, and customer experience in retail stores.

Inventory Specialist

Manage stock levels, track inventory, and ensure timely replenishment of products.

Customer Service Representative

Assist customers with inquiries, complaints, and purchase decisions in retail settings.

Visual Merchandiser

Design and arrange product displays to attract customers and enhance sales.

Sales Associate

Engage with customers, promote products, and assist in achieving sales targets.

Supply Chain Analyst

Analyze supply chain processes to improve efficiency and reduce costs in retail operations.

Retail Operations Manager

Develop and implement strategies to streamline retail operations and improve profitability.

Category Manager

Oversee product categories, pricing strategies, and promotions to maximize sales.

Loss Prevention Specialist

Prevent theft, fraud, and losses in retail stores through monitoring and training.

Retail Buyer

Source and purchase products for retail stores based on market trends and customer demand.

Training Coordinator

Develop and deliver training programs for retail staff to enhance skills and performance.

E-commerce Integration Specialist

Bridge the gap between physical stores and online platforms for seamless retail operations.
