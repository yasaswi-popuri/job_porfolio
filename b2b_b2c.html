B2B & B2C Sales Field

B2B (Business-to-Business) and B2C (Business-to-Consumer) Sales involve building relationships, understanding customer needs, and driving revenue through strategic selling. These fields are critical for business growth, market expansion, and customer satisfaction. Freshers earn ₹3–8 lakh, mid-level ₹8–15 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹15–30 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹50 lakh annually.

Available Roles

Sales Manager

Lead and manage a team of sales professionals to achieve revenue targets.

Account Executive

Build and maintain relationships with clients to drive sales and retention.

Inside Sales Representative

Engage with customers remotely to promote products and close deals.

Field Sales Executive

Meet clients in person to build relationships and secure sales agreements.

Key Account Manager

Manage high-value accounts and ensure long-term partnerships with key clients.

Sales Development Representative (SDR)

Identify and qualify leads to support the sales pipeline.

Customer Success Manager

Ensure customer satisfaction and retention by addressing their needs and challenges.

Retail Sales Associate

Assist customers in-store to enhance their shopping experience and drive sales.

Digital Sales Specialist

Leverage online platforms and tools to promote products and generate leads.

Channel Sales Manager

Develop partnerships with distributors and resellers to expand market reach.

E-commerce Sales Strategist

Create and implement strategies to boost online sales performance.

Sales Analyst

Analyze sales data to identify trends, optimize strategies, and improve outcomes.
