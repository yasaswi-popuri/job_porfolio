Cloud Computing & DevOps Field

Cloud Computing and DevOps involve managing cloud infrastructure, automating workflows, and ensuring seamless deployment processes. These fields are critical for modern software development and operations. Freshers earn ₹4–12 lakh, mid-level ₹12–25 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹25–60 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹1 crore annually.

Available Roles

Cloud Engineer

Design and manage cloud infrastructure using AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Proficiency in cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud).
  • Experience with infrastructure as code (Terraform, CloudFormation).
  • Understanding of networking and security principles.

DevOps Engineer

Automate deployment pipelines and streamline development workflows.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Experience with CI/CD tools (Jenkins, GitLab CI).
  • Proficiency in scripting languages (Bash, Python).
  • Understanding of containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).

Site Reliability Engineer

Ensure system reliability and performance through automation and monitoring.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Experience with monitoring tools (Prometheus, Grafana).
  • Proficiency in incident management and root cause analysis.
  • Strong problem-solving skills.

Cloud Architect

Design scalable cloud solutions and oversee their implementation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Experience with cloud architecture design patterns.
  • Proficiency in multi-cloud environments (AWS, Azure, GCP).
