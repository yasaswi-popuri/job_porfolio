Investment Banking

Investment Banking involves financial advisory, mergers and acquisitions, capital raising, and asset management. These fields are critical for driving economic growth and helping businesses achieve their strategic goals. Freshers earn ₹6–15 lakh, mid-level ₹15–30 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹30–80 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹2 crore annually.

Available Roles

Investment Banker

Advise clients on financial strategies, mergers, acquisitions, and capital markets.

Equity Research Analyst

Analyze stocks, industries, and market trends to provide investment recommendations.

Mergers & Acquisitions Specialist

Facilitate mergers, acquisitions, and divestitures for corporate clients.

Private Equity Analyst

Evaluate investment opportunities in private companies and manage portfolios.

Venture Capitalist

Invest in startups and early-stage companies with high growth potential.

Financial Analyst

Assess financial data, prepare reports, and support decision-making processes.

Corporate Finance Manager

Manage financial planning, budgeting, and capital structure for corporations.

Hedge Fund Manager

Oversee investment funds that use advanced strategies to maximize returns.

Wealth Manager

Provide personalized financial advice and portfolio management for high-net-worth individuals.

Risk Analyst

Evaluate financial risks and develop strategies to mitigate them.

Capital Markets Analyst

Work on raising capital through equity, debt, or hybrid instruments.

Asset Manager

Manage investment portfolios and ensure optimal returns for clients.
