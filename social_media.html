Social Media Management Field

Social Media Management involves creating, curating, and managing content across digital platforms to engage audiences, build brand awareness, and drive business growth. Freshers earn ₹3–8 lakh, mid-level ₹8–15 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹15–30 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹50 lakh annually.

Available Roles

Social Media Manager

Develop and execute strategies to grow brand presence and engagement on social platforms.

Content Creator

Produce engaging posts, videos, and graphics tailored for social media audiences.

Community Manager

Engage with followers, respond to comments, and foster a positive online community.

Social Media Analyst

Analyze data and metrics to optimize campaigns and improve performance.

Influencer Manager

Collaborate with influencers to promote brands and products effectively.

Digital Marketing Specialist

Create and implement paid ad campaigns across social media platforms.

Video Editor

Edit video content for platforms like Instagram Reels, YouTube, and TikTok.

Copywriter

Write compelling captions, scripts, and ad copy for social media posts.

Social Media Strategist

Design long-term strategies to align social media efforts with business goals.

SEO Specialist

Optimize social media content for search engines to increase visibility.

Graphic Designer

Create visually appealing graphics and designs for social media campaigns.

Campaign Manager

Plan, execute, and monitor large-scale social media campaigns.
