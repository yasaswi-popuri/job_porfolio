Film & Television Production

Film and Television Production involve creating visual content for movies, TV shows, documentaries, and other media formats. These fields require creativity, technical skills, and collaboration across various roles. Freshers earn ₹3–8 lakh, mid-level professionals ₹8–15 lakh, and experienced individuals ₹15–30 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹50 lakh annually.

Available Roles

Director

Oversee the creative vision of a film or TV project, guiding actors and crew.

Producer

Manage the business and logistics of production, including budgeting and scheduling.

Screenwriter

Write scripts and develop storylines for films, TV shows, or web series.

Cinematographer

Handle camera work and lighting to capture the visual aesthetics of a project.

Editor

Assemble raw footage into a cohesive final product using editing software.

Sound Designer

Create and integrate audio elements like sound effects and music into productions.

Production Designer

Design sets, costumes, and props to bring the director's vision to life.

Visual Effects Artist

Create digital effects and animations to enhance scenes in post-production.

Lighting Technician

Set up and operate lighting equipment to achieve desired visual effects.

Location Manager

Scout and manage filming locations, ensuring smooth operations during shoots.

Script Supervisor

Ensure continuity and accuracy in dialogue, actions, and props during filming.

Post-Production Supervisor

Oversee the editing, sound mixing, and final delivery of a project.
