Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Electrical & Electronics Engineering involves designing, developing, and maintaining electrical systems and electronic devices. This field is critical for power generation, automation, telecommunications, and consumer electronics. Freshers earn ₹3–8 lakh, mid-level professionals ₹8–20 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹20–50 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹1 crore annually.

Available Roles

Electrical Engineer

Design, develop, and test electrical equipment and systems for various applications.

Electronics Engineer

Work on the design and development of electronic circuits, devices, and systems.

Control Systems Engineer

Develop and implement control systems for industrial automation and robotics.

Power Systems Engineer

Design and maintain systems for power generation, transmission, and distribution.

Telecommunications Engineer

Design and optimize communication systems, including wireless networks and satellite systems.

Embedded Systems Engineer

Develop embedded software and hardware for electronic devices and IoT applications.

Automation Engineer

Implement automated systems for manufacturing and process industries.

Renewable Energy Engineer

Focus on designing and implementing sustainable energy solutions like solar and wind power.

VLSI Design Engineer

Design and develop integrated circuits and semiconductor devices.

Signal Processing Engineer

Analyze and manipulate signals for applications in audio, video, and communication systems.

Robotics Engineer

Design and build robots for industrial, medical, and consumer applications.

Instrumentation Engineer

Design and maintain instruments and control systems for industrial processes.
