Environmental & Renewable Energy Engineering

Environmental and Renewable Energy Engineering focuses on sustainable practices and technologies to address climate change, energy efficiency, and resource conservation. Freshers earn ₹4–12 lakh, mid-level professionals ₹12–30 lakh, and experienced engineers ₹30–70 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹1 crore annually.

Available Roles

Solar Engineer

Design and implement solar energy systems for residential, commercial, or industrial use.

Wind Energy Specialist

Develop and maintain wind turbines and wind farms for clean energy generation.

Environmental Consultant

Advise organizations on environmental regulations, sustainability, and green practices.

Energy Auditor

Evaluate energy usage in buildings and recommend efficiency improvements.

Hydrologist

Study water resources and manage water-related projects like dams, irrigation, and flood control.

Biomass Engineer

Work on bioenergy systems that convert organic materials into energy sources.

Geothermal Engineer

Design systems to harness heat from the Earth's core for heating or electricity generation.

Waste Management Specialist

Develop strategies for waste reduction, recycling, and sustainable disposal.

Renewable Energy Analyst

Analyze data to optimize renewable energy systems and improve performance.

Climate Change Analyst

Assess the impact of climate change and propose mitigation strategies.

Green Building Engineer

Design eco-friendly buildings with energy-efficient systems and sustainable materials.

Water Resource Engineer

Plan and manage projects related to water supply, treatment, and distribution.
