State Civil Services

State Civil Services play a vital role in governance and administration at the state level. These roles involve managing public resources, ensuring law and order, implementing government policies, and serving citizens. Entry-level positions start at ₹5–10 lakh annually, mid-level professionals earn ₹10–20 lakh, and senior officers can earn ₹20–50 lakh or more.
