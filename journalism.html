Journalism & Broadcasting Field

Journalism and Broadcasting involve gathering, analyzing, and presenting news and information to the public through various media platforms. These fields are essential for informing, educating, and entertaining audiences. Freshers earn ₹3–8 lakh, mid-level professionals ₹8–15 lakh, and experienced individuals ₹15–30 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹50 lakh annually.

Available Roles

News Reporter

Cover breaking news stories, conduct interviews, and write articles for print or digital media.

Broadcast Journalist

Research, write, and present news stories for television, radio, or online platforms.

Editor

Review, edit, and approve content for accuracy, grammar, and style before publication.

Anchor/Presenter

Host news programs, talk shows, or live events, engaging with audiences and guests.

Photojournalist

Capture compelling images and videos to accompany news stories and features.

Content Writer

Create engaging written content for websites, blogs, and social media platforms.

Producer

Plan and oversee the production of news segments, documentaries, or broadcasts.

Social Media Manager

Manage and grow a media organization's presence on social media platforms.

Video Editor

Edit raw footage into polished video content for news or entertainment purposes.

Radio Jockey (RJ)

Host radio shows, engage with listeners, and curate music playlists.

Public Relations Officer

Manage communication between organizations and the public to build a positive image.

Data Journalist

Analyze and visualize data to uncover trends and tell impactful stories.
