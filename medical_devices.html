Medical Devices & Equipment Field

The Medical Devices & Equipment field involves designing, manufacturing, and maintaining tools and technologies used in healthcare. These fields are critical for improving patient care, diagnostics, and treatment outcomes. Freshers earn ₹3–8 lakh, mid-level ₹8–15 lakh, and experienced professionals ₹15–30 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹50 lakh annually.

Available Roles

Biomedical Engineer

Design and develop medical devices and equipment for healthcare applications.

Product Manager

Oversee the development and lifecycle of medical devices and equipment.

Regulatory Affairs Specialist

Ensure compliance with regulations for medical device approvals.

Quality Assurance Engineer

Test and ensure the quality of medical devices before they reach the market.

Field Service Engineer

Maintain and repair medical equipment at healthcare facilities.

R&D Engineer

Research and develop innovative medical devices and technologies.

Technical Support Specialist

Provide technical assistance and troubleshooting for medical devices.

Clinical Applications Specialist

Train healthcare professionals on the use of medical devices.

Manufacturing Engineer

Optimize the production process for medical devices and equipment.

Sales Representative

Promote and sell medical devices to hospitals and clinics.

Supply Chain Manager

Manage the logistics and distribution of medical devices and equipment.

Medical Device Tester

Conduct rigorous testing to ensure safety and functionality of devices.
