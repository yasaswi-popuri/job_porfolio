Veterinary & Animal Health

Veterinary and Animal Health focus on providing medical care, research, and support for animals. This field includes clinical practice, animal welfare, diagnostics, and public health initiatives. Freshers earn ₹3–7 lakh, mid-level professionals ₹7–15 lakh, and experienced veterinarians ₹15–30 lakh. Senior roles can exceed ₹50 lakh annually.

Available Roles

Veterinarian

Provide medical care and treatment for animals in clinics or farms.

Veterinary Surgeon

Perform surgeries on animals to treat injuries and diseases.

Animal Nutritionist

Develop dietary plans to improve the health and performance of animals.

Veterinary Technician

Assist veterinarians with diagnostics, treatments, and surgeries.

Zoo Veterinarian

Care for animals in zoos and wildlife sanctuaries.

Animal Welfare Officer

Ensure the ethical treatment and well-being of animals in various settings.

Veterinary Pathologist

Study diseases in animals through laboratory testing and diagnosis.

Livestock Consultant

Advise farmers on animal health, breeding, and management practices.

Pet Behavior Specialist

Help pet owners address behavioral issues in their animals.

Veterinary Researcher

Conduct research to advance animal health and veterinary science.

Wildlife Veterinarian

Provide medical care to wild animals and assist in conservation efforts.

Veterinary Pharmacist

Develop and dispense medications for animal health.
